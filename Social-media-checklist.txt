Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

Do's
* Use a professional profile picture and bio
* Keep posts respectful and positive
* Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts
* Review privacy settings regularly
* Think before posting anything personal
* Change passwords every 2 months
* Keep a strong but rememberable password

Don'ts
* Don't share your location, phone number or college ID
* Don't post about exams, internal marks or college matters
* Don't argue, bully or spread negativity online
* Don't accept friend requests from unknown people
* Don't share passwords, OTPs or sensitive information
